You're standing at the door. Behind you, the orchard. In front of you — eleven hundred square feet of glass, wood, and mountain light.

Villa Medan is a private A-frame cottage at 3,200 feet in the Kashmir Valley, surrounded by 1.2 acres of working apple orchard. Step inside.

The Living Room

Cathedral ceilings,
floor-to-roof glass.

The A-frame opens into a double-height living space — exposed beams, warm timber, and a glass wall that frames the orchard like a painting that changes every hour.

The Master Bedroom

Sleep with the
orchard outside.

The ground-floor master opens directly onto the garden. Wake at first light to apple trees in the window and birdsong as your alarm clock.

The Loft

An upstairs
perch.

A spiral stair leads to the loft bedroom — a triangular window perfectly framing the valley. The most-photographed corner of the property.

Kitchen & Dining

Gather around
the table.

Exposed brick, warm wood, a table long enough for everyone you love. Optional Kashmiri chef on request — wazwan dinners are a guest favourite.

The Garden

Afternoons are
yours.

Noon chai under the apple trees. Picnic spreads on local rugs. The garden is a stage — the orchard, your audience.

Evenings

Amber light,
open skies.

As the sun dips behind the Pir Panjal, the A-frame glows from within. Stars emerge without competition — no city light reaches this altitude.

The Fire Circle

Stories around
the fire.

Stone fire pit, Adirondack chairs for six, firewood provided year-round. Above you, the kind of sky most people only see in postcards.

The Pool

Sun, water,
and blue sky.

A private mosaic pool in the heart of the orchard — a rare luxury at altitude. May through October, the warmest spot on the property.

What's included

Every detail, considered.

1,100 sq ft glass A-frame with cathedral ceilings and panoramic orchard views. Sleeps 4 adults.

Two Bedrooms

Glass-walled master plus an upper loft with valley window. Luxury Kashmiri linens throughout.

Private Pool

Mosaic-tiled pool surrounded by apple trees. Open May–October. Loungers and stone deck included.

Fire Circle

Stone fire pit with Adirondack seating for 6. Firewood provided. Unbeatable night-sky setting.

Full Kitchen

Open kitchen, espresso machine, dining for 6. Optional Kashmiri chef at ₹2,500/day.

High-Speed WiFi

Satellite internet across the property at 50+ Mbps. Work from paradise.

Orchard Access

Full access to 1.2 acres of working apple orchard. Pick fruit in season. Total privacy.

Concierge

24/7 host support, local guides, shikara bookings on Dal Lake, curated Pahalgam experiences.
